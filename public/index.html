About us

Urban meals , is a start up aiming at the foodie population to discover eateries of your choice around the town. The app will aid to explore popular yet pocket friendly restaurants, featuring the highlights of the menu so that you can get tummy filled in affordable expense. Let your taste buds feel some goosebumps by finding all way to choose the best for tasting the best.

Presenting novel food trends among the urban circle, is an application in role to ease food navigation. This helps the foodie in you to choose and have your favourite cuisine with a single tap. Binding up all the elements to meet with the adequecies for a food hunt, Urban meals provides with myriad options to engage a foodie as a whole through food reviews, blogging and other social connections.
